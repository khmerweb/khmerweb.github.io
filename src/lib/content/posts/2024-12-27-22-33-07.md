Chu Sinan wanted to be a dancer for his entire life. He has a partner that can help him achieve his ambitions. After an accident, he is devastated to discover that she had been struggling with many issues, emotionally and physically, and temporarily leaves dance. So, he takes on a young student and grooms her to replace Lilly. He did not count on the emotional void he feels without Lilly by his side.
